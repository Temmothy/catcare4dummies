Our mission is to help and support new cat owners globally! For questions, advice, or a helping paw, Cat Care 4 Dummies is always here for you. Whether you're a first-time kitten parent or just need guidance on the basics, our team is ready to assist. You can reach us by phone or send us an email. We'll be sure to get back to you as soon as possible.

We’re based in Chicago, IL, but our support and resources are available to cat lovers everywhere. Don’t hesitate to get in touch—we’re here to make cat care simple, stress-free, and a little more fun!
